About

// 3D · VFX

I'm from Coimbra, where I studied Multimedia at the Instituto Superior Miguel Torga. From there I spent two years at the Blachere Illumination Design Studio — the studio behind the Blachere Illumination Group, based in Coimbra, Portugal, at the Instituto Pedro Nunes. I joined as an intern and stayed on for a second year, developing 3D festive decoration pieces and working closely with the team to take each design from concept to finished model.

My tool is Cinema 4D, and the part of the craft I care about most is simulation — Pyro, particles and fluids — alongside lighting, shading and getting the final render to read exactly the way it should. That's where I want to grow: building more technically demanding simulation work and pushing the quality of every render as far as it can go.
